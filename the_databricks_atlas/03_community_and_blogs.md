# Community & Blogs: Join the Conversation

This page highlights the best places to connect with the Databricks community, ask questions, and stay up-to-date with the latest trends and best practices.

## Official Community Hubs

- **[Databricks Community](https://community.databricks.com/)**: The official Databricks community forum. A great place to ask questions, share knowledge, and connect with other users.
- **[Databricks Blog](https://www.databricks.com/blog)**: The official Databricks blog, featuring product announcements, technical articles, and customer stories.
- **[Databricks Technical Blog](https://community.databricks.com/t5/technical-blog/bg-p/technical-blog)**: A more technical blog from the Databricks community, with deep dives into various topics.

## Top Independent Blogs

- **[The Databricks Blog by Denny Lee](https://www.dennylee.com/databricks)**: A fantastic blog from a Databricks employee, with a wealth of information on Spark, Delta Lake, and more.
- **[Koalas to Pandas](https://koalas.readthedocs.io/en/latest/)**: The official documentation for the Koalas library, which provides a pandas-like API for Spark.

## Community Forums

- **[Stack Overflow (databricks tag)](https://stackoverflow.com/questions/tagged/databricks)**: The go-to place for technical questions and answers.
- **[Reddit (r/databricks)](https://www.reddit.com/r/databricks/)**: A growing community for discussing all things Databricks.
