# Key Conference Talks: Insights from the Experts

This page curates some of the most impactful and insightful talks from the Data + AI Summit and other major conferences. These presentations provide a deeper understanding of the concepts, best practices, and future direction of the data and AI landscape.

## Data + AI Summit 2023

- **[Keynote: The Future of Data and AI](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=...)**: A must-watch keynote from the Databricks leadership team, outlining the vision for the data lakehouse and the future of generative AI.
- **[Deep Dive into Delta Lake 3.0](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=...)**: An in-depth technical session on the latest features and performance improvements in Delta Lake 3.0.
- **[Building Production-Ready LLM Apps](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=...)**: A practical guide to building and deploying Large Language Model applications on Databricks.

## Other Notable Talks

- **[The Past, Present, and Future of Apache Spark (Spark Summit)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=...)**: A talk by Matei Zaharia, the creator of Spark, on the evolution and future of the project.
- **[Data Mesh in Practice (various conferences)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=...)**: A collection of talks on the practical implementation of the Data Mesh architecture, a key concept in modern data strategy.
