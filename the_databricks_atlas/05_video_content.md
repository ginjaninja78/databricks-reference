# Video Content: Learn by Watching

This page provides a curated list of the best video resources for learning Databricks, from official channels to community content.

## Official Databricks Channels

- **[Databricks YouTube Channel](https://www.youtube.com/c/Databricks)**: The official YouTube channel for Databricks, with a wealth of content including product demos, tutorials, and customer stories.
- **[Data + AI Summit](https://www.youtube.com/c/DataAISummit)**: The YouTube channel for the annual Data + AI Summit, with recordings of all the keynotes and sessions.

## Recommended Community Channels

- **[Advancing Analytics](https://www.youtube.com/c/AdvancingAnalytics)**: A great channel with a focus on the Microsoft data platform, including a lot of content on Azure Databricks.
- **[Guy in a Cube](https://www.youtube.com/c/GuyinaCube)**: While focused on Power BI, this channel has some excellent content on connecting Power BI to Databricks.
